More information about us:

Long Bay College is a co-educational decile 10 school located in Torbay, Auckland, New Zealand. At Long Bay College, you'll find a team of expert teachers and highly capable administrators. This builds the foundation for students at Long Bay College to grow into young, refined individuals that will achieve highly and thrive in cultures of respect and support. Long Bay College offers a wide range of curriculum options, excelling in Academia, The Arts, Culture, and Sport. Our courses are designed to directly lead into further learning, such as an apprenticeship or a University degree. Considering our focus on a holistic education, we value extracurricular activities significantly, seeking the highest quality of tutors, coaches and mentors to create an emphasis on developing the skills or talents of our students within the community.
